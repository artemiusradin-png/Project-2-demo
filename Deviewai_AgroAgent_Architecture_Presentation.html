deviewai × AgroPlatformaThe Reasoning
2026-05-20 · Rev C
Chosen for the customer, not for us

Why not Agro-Markets first?

The trap
B is the most exciting to pitch — "what to plant, when to sell." But a market prediction is unfalsifiable until a season passes.
The principle
The customer's #1 goal is to build trust in the AI. You earn that with answers that prove themselves today — a leaf diagnosis, a reconcilable quote.

B needs new external integrations and can't be validated with a held-out test set. It belongs in Phase 2, after the flywheel is proven — high value, wrong order.
