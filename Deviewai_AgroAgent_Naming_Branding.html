Purpose. A naming and brand architecture for the multi-agent AI suite. One umbrella — Agro Agent — with a coherent family of five named agents. The naming logic is deliberate: a shared Agro- prefix ties the suite to AgroPlatforma and Agro-Arena, and each agent owns a single, plain-language capability word. Names are EN/UA-friendly and survive transliteration to Cyrillic.

The Umbrella — Agro Agent

Agro Agent™ is the suite brand: the AI layer that lives inside AgroPlatforma. Not a chatbot bolted on — a set of focused agents, each owning one job, sharing one foundation. Suite line: “Five agents. One field-to-deal loop.”

Agro-Vision
The flagship — and the v1 product.
Photo → diagnosis → prescription from the catalog, in seconds. The agent that proves the suite and builds the trust the other four inherit.
▸ v1 ships Agro-Vision + the shared foundation. Agents B–E attach to it at a fraction of the cost.

Naming Logic

  • Agro- prefix — locks every agent to the AgroPlatforma / Agro-Arena parent brand; instantly legible to a Ukrainian agronomist.
  • Single capability word — Vision, Markets, Flow, Mentor, Insight. No invented words, no acronyms; each name is the value proposition.
  • Cyrillic-safe — Агро-Віжн, Агро-Маркетс, Агро-Флоу, Агро-Ментор, Агро-Інсайт all read cleanly.
  • Hierarchy — “Agro Agent” is the suite; the five are agents within it, never standalone competing brands.

Agent Identities

Agro-Vision
Field Intelligence · Block A
v1
“Photo to prescription in five seconds.”
The eye in the field. Identifies crop, pest, weed or disease from a photo and returns a ranked, in-stock, Ukraine-approved prescription with agronomist commentary. The trust-builder for the whole suite.
Agro-Markets
Commercial Intelligence · Block B
P2
“What to plant. When to sell.”
The trading brain. Reads past harvests, rotation and local + global price trends to advise what to sow next season and when to move grain. Turns a catalog into a plan.
Agro-Flow
Operational Intelligence · Block C
P2
“Quotes and orders, without the desk.”
The tireless operator. Auto-builds quotes in Salesforce, finds analogues for up- and cross-sell, automates order entry. Lets the business grow without growing the operations team.
Agro-Mentor
Network Intelligence · Block D
P3
“Every consultant at top-agronomist level.”
The coach. Runs the task marketplace and a gamified learning track — matching jobs to a consultant's rating and teaching from their own past mistakes and vendor material.
Agro-Insight
Vendor Intelligence · Block E
P3
“Live answers for every supplier.”
The analyst. Personalised, live reports and dashboards for vendors — regional performance, focus consultants, problem hotspots — designed for the not-very-digital supplier rep.
Agro Agent
The Suite · Umbrella
“Five agents. One field-to-deal loop.”
The parent brand. Always “Agro Agent” for the system; agents are referred to as “Agro-Vision” etc. Never pluralise the agents into rival products.
