Before sending — pre-flight

  • Complete doc 05 (Team Experience). Fill the yellow ▸ TO FILL blocks: 2–4 project references with verifiable outcomes, named team members. Mary's rule: specifics or omit.
  • Confirm doc 02 (Pricing) is current. Standard v1 quote of $105k, ~12 weeks, payment 30/40/30.
  • Re-read doc 06 (Open Questions). Decisions D1 (RAG corpus ownership) and D2 (Flutter app constraints) are the build-start blockers — flag them in your cover note.
  • Strip the internal companion. Doc 07 (Talk-Track) stays with you. Do not attach it.

How to send

  • As HTML: zip the six client docs from the project root and send. Each opens in any modern browser; no build step needed.
  • As PDF: open each doc, print → Save as PDF. Page sizes and breaks are already configured (A4 for documents, landscape for the deck). Combine into one PDF in this order if desired.
  • For the call: open doc 01 (Presentation) in fullscreen (F); keep doc 07 (Talk-Track) on a second screen or printout.
